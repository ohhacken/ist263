Dolphins

⭐⭐☆☆☆☆☆☆☆☆

Facts:

  1. They’re marine mammals, so they aren’t fish
  2. They breathe through the blowhole on the top of their heads
  3. They can communicate by squeaking and whistling
  4. They’re mega predators and can eat over thirty-three pounds of fish a day
  5. They like to kill baby porpoises just for the sake of doing it
  6. Male dolphins will kill baby dolphins to mate with female dolphins
  7. They’ve been observed mating with dead things

My Opinions:

While dolphins make cute sounds and their tricks can be adorable, they're big jerks. They should be recognized as the scary marine mammals they are.
