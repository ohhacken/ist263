Sharks

Facts:

  1. A lot of species of sharks are endangered
  2. You’re 30 times more likely to be killed by a falling coconut than a shark
  3. In their lifetime they can lose more than 30,000 teeth
  4. Depending on the species sharks can give birth in three different ways
  5. Their skin is more like their teeth than fish scales, this allows them to swim faster
  6. They don’t have bones
  7. They have organs that can sense electromagnetic fields

My Opinions:

Shark's teeth can definitely be off putting but they're still super cool. They're not as dangerous to humans as hollywood would have you believe.

Tarantulas:

Facts:

  1. There are over a hundred species of tarantulas
  2. They eat insects, frogs, mice, and toads
  3. The live underground in burrows
  4. They can secrete an enzyme that liquifies their prey’s bodies
  5. Their venom has a low toxicity to humans
  6. They can live up to 30 years

My Opinions:

I hate tarantulas. They're creepy and I don't care if they can't really hurt me. They disturb me to the core of my very being and are objectively the most terrifying thing I've seen in my life.
